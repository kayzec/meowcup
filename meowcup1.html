About Meowcup


Operating since August 24, 2021. Unfortunately we are in hiatus

The Meowcup was founded by a Student, Kim Chi, in hoping by creating Meowcup to revive the vintage cake design promises to evoke a delightful nostalgia, blending classic aesthetics with delectable flavors for a charming culinary experience. A slice of vintage cake, adorned with delicate lace patterns of vanilla frosting, unfolds layers of rich, moist decadence, whispering tales of timeless flavors.

Currently studying in college, as a student it is hard to manage a business while studying. Thereafter enrolling I decided to stop my business until I graduated. Hoping you'll all wait for me, I'll study hard to ensure you the finest cake you'll ever see. Mina-san, hontou ni arigatou gozaimasu! See you in 2025
